Game Collecting - Scott The Woz

I found Scott the Woz earlier this year I think. (2020 has been so long I had to check, but, yeah, 9 months ago) If you haven't seen any of his videos, watching this one is a great introduction to Scott's wholesome content style. His videos typically switch between 3 categories: Game Reviews, Long Form Sketches, and Game-Related Topic Video Essays The latter is what this video is, where Scott picks some topic and discusses it. While it is a simple premise, I find Scott to be incredible at sticking to interesting topics and being smart about which aspects to delve deeper into. With this video in particular, Scott discusses the hobby of collecting video games and shows off his collection quite a bit. I found this video really interesting, especially with the rare items Scott shows towards the end of the video. I highly recommend giving this one a look.

Sub Focus & Wilkinson - Enter Night

Okay, I realize I've pulled songs from Sub Focus and Wilkinson's Portals album twice already, but this song is just another one I've had on repeat that I felt I had to share. Enter Night was the first of the new tracks on the album (not singles) that I listened to and it is such a great start to the album (2nd track on the album). It feels very relaxed in the beginning and slowly builds with a catchy melody into the drop. I've found myself coming back to the song over and over again within the past month so I'd highly recommend you give that a listen and, if you haven't yet and you like this one, give the rest of the album a try too!
